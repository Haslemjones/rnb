WHO WE ARE

We are (RNB) Real Naija Boys a brotherhood of six Nigerian boys who started as strangers united by a shared passion for streaming and entertainment. Scattered across different corners of the world, we came together to form a creative powerhouse with one purpose: to represent and celebrate our African, specifically Nigerian, culture in the most authentic way possible. What began as late-night gaming sessions and casual online conversations quickly evolved into something deeper. Our bond grew beyond the screen we became brothers. We didn't just share a love for entertainment; we shared a vision, a mission to build something bigger than ourselves. Together, we are shaping the future of African entertainment, one project at a time.
