Who We Are

We are (RNB) Real Naija Boys a brotherhood of six Nigerian boys who started as strangers united by a shared passion for streaming and entertainment. Scattered across different corners of the world, we came together to form a creative powerhouse with one purpose: to represent and celebrate our African, specifically Nigerian, culture in the most authentic way possible.
